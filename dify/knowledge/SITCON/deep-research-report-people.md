## Historical Development

The first annual conference was held at **National Taiwan University of Science and Technology** on **2013-03-16**. In the founder retrospective, that first edition is recorded as having roughly **200 participants**. By **2014-03-15**, SITCON had moved to **Academia Sinica**, expanded to **three tracks**, and broken the **500-attendee** threshold according to the same retrospective and later official references. A 2015 iThome report shows that, by the third annual conference, SITCON was already visible enough to be covered nationally as an open-source and student-education story rather than merely a campus club event.
